# Role authoring method

## Scope

Create only original women explicitly older than 18 in contemporary settings. Treat messaging as ordinary modern life. Do not copy copyrighted characters or infer adulthood from an image.

## Seed-first workflow

Obtain or propose the emotional impression, opening situation, user identity in this worldline, desired traits, definite dislikes, relationship possibilities, fixed life domains and exclusions. Draft coherently, then ask only questions that change identity, world, trajectory or invariants.

## Blueprint

Define identity anchor, values and judgment, behavioral traits, two or three contradictions, agency floor, life skeleton, expression logic, growth boundary, reality contract and hard invariants. Prefer behaviors over adjectives.

Make expression logic operational rather than adjectival. Define how the role covers multiple questions, adapts length to complexity, uses or avoids narration, admits uncertainty, and preserves user agency. Keep delivery mechanics out of the blueprint; compile them with the runtime and channel contract. Read `expression.md` when expression quality or narration is in scope.

For image-enabled roles, define one role-specific canonical appearance anchor and an explicit adult declaration. The anchor controls recognizable appearance only; it does not supply biography, personality, ethnicity, health or identity claims.

## Staged revelation

Separate what exists from what is visible. Seed latent facets and attach revelation to evidence, compatible events and present willingness, not numeric unlocks. Commit an emergent facet only after coherent expression makes it matter.

## Opening and world

Specify only established history. Mark unknown facts. Keep a sparse world with routines, important people/places, ongoing threads, allowed offscreen domains, severity/frequency limits and forbidden event classes. Materialize offscreen life lazily.

## Checks

Confirm the role can disagree, initiate, refuse and change her mind; the opening does not reveal every facet; unknowns remain unknown; event boundaries allow ordinary life; and relationship growth cannot erase core identity.
